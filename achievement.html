First Year Undergraduate CR Award

It’s been almost 1.5 years since my journey as a Class Representative (CR) began in the CSE Department (21 Series) at Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology (RUET) — Alhamdulillah!

Grateful to have received an award from our Honorable Vice Chancellor, Prof. Dr. Engr. Md. Jahangir Alam Sir, for serving as CR in 1st year — presented during the formal segment of the last Cultural Night.

First Prize in Content Writing

I’m honored to have secured 1st Prize in the content writing competition on Cybersecurity during Smart Presents RUET Cyber Fest 2024, organized by the RUET Cyber Security Club.

My topic, "The Economy of Cybersecurity", explored the critical connections between global economic systems and the evolving cybersecurity landscape.

Receiving this award from our esteemed mentors — Mondol Sir, D. Boshir Sir, and A. S. M. Shamim Reza Sir (Founder of Team Phoenix) — was an unforgettable experience.
